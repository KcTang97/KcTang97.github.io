Keep It Up, 1024p x 740p, Code Game, 2019

The play of this game is to avoid getting hit by books.

The books represented the workflow that we have to overcome in order to graduate.

The purpose of having the flashing background and motion blur is to make player's eyes get tired

Just like our eyes get tired after staring at the digital devices and books for long period of time.
