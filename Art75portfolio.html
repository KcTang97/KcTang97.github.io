"Heart Window", Digital Vdieo, 3 min, 2018

I used the darkroom to symbolize our/my inner heart, the window as the sight.

I want the audience to experience the two directly opposite world with the soundtrack.

Experience the world of an introvert, people with trauma or autism live.

It is not a work to express negative emotion about these group to the viewer,

but to have a different perspective to be able to see the different side of them, a relax and free side.







"Fire", Digital Video 2 min, 2018

WIldfire one of the common disaster caused by us human, only people who live around know how cruel and horrible it is.

Fire, it does keep coldness away from us, but it does more harm than just keep us warm, wildfire is the best answer.

I want to express the seriousness of wildfire, and maybe warning people the harmfulness of fire.
