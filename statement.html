Artist Statement

My art practice is to explore new media incorporate with the limited knowledge and skills I learned from classes.

During the processes of making these digital art pieces, I slowly realize my goals.

I like to explore the things that we commonly pay less attention like the projects I have done in Art 75 and 74.

However, I create works based on curiosity and interest in the medium and how it results differently than others.

I like to compose any inspirations that flash in my head. It maybe comes from the news or events that I experienced or the emotion of a memory scene.

But, I want to create something personal, and my perspective of the world.

Therefore, in 2019, the projects I have done, I try to implement personal elements and events that I experienced in the works for the experiment.

I continue to develop my style digitally after the critiques and lessons from classes.

The goals of my works mostly are to experiment with each different medium that I don't familiar with and use them to express my emotional world and my ideology.
