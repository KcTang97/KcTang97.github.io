Heart Window, 1920 x 1080, Digital Vdieo, 2 min 56 sec, 2018

I used the darkroom to symbolize our/my inner heart, the window as the sight.

I want the audience to experience the two directly opposite world with the soundtrack.

Experience the world of an introvert, people with trauma or autism live.

It is not a work to express negative emotion about these groups to the viewer,

but to have a different perspective to be able to see the different sides of them, a relaxed and free side.







Fire, 1920 x 1080, Digital Video 2 min 12 sec, 2018

Wildfire, one of the common disasters caused by us humans, only people who experienced know its crucial and pain.

Fire, it keeps coldness away from us, but it is dangerous and harmful, wildfire is the best answer.

I want to express the seriousness of wildfire and remind people of the harmfulness of fire.

Dream, 1920 x 1080, Digtal drawing and animation, 1 min 22 sec, 2019

This project is about a dream I have had in many years ago,

Virus series, 1920 x 1080, Digtal Animation, 3 min 30 sec, 2020

The animation is a series I am creating specifically for the period we live in now. In this series,

I am using both AfterEffect and Photoshop for the entire series due to my artist practice.

The content of this is to explore the emotion and reaction of people from everyday news reports we seem on the television and network.

Also, the perspective of people like me viewing this health crisis.

The style of the animation inspires by the idea of flipbook, which is the very first simple animation I interacted with in my childhood.
