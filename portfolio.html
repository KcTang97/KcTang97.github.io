Heart Window, 2018

I used the darkroom to symbolize our/my inner heart, the window as the sight. I want the audience to experience the two directly opposite world with the soundtrack. Experience the world of an introvert, people with trauma or autism live. It is not a work to express negative emotion about these group to the viewer, but to have a different perspective to be able to see the different side of them, a relax and free side.
