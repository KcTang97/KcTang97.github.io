"Dream", Digtal drawing and animation, 1 min 22 sec, 2019

This project is about a dream I have had in many years ago,

and I still remember one of the incredible scenes, it is so beautiful and unforgettable, so I decided to animate it.

Because the dream was a long time ago and I only remember the last part of it,

so I choose to go along with the concept of family moments, the happy moments.

Everything that is animated is the sketches I made through Photoshop.

For the ending of my animation, I take the idea from class critique in the previous class, so it ends better and emotion.
